True Story: Suicide, as a result of depression and anxiety is the second biggest killer of young people (aged 15-29) globally.


Good News: Mental illness doesn’t always have to kill…not when you can do something about it!


We are global movement of young Africans working together, for the next Africa, an Africa with a sound mind!

Join Us Now

90% of Africans with mental illness have no access to treatment.

They lack knowledge of their conditions and sometimes cannot even express what they are going through. Even they can, they are victims of judgment, stigma and alienation.

Here’s our plan to flip the script.

The Power of Arts

We are educating young people and creating awareness about mental health through inspiring stories, drama and art therapies.

Learn More
Championing Advocates

We are assembling a global community of young people passionate about helping people with mental illness.

Learn More
Tech for Good

We are leveraging on technology to inform and give mental health care to everyone, even the hardest to reach.
